Studio life.

You feel it on your first day, while you meet the rest of the team members who want to know about your trip, YOUR career. You feel it in your first week, as you quickly dive into an endless race (personalized with your name) over the vision for our next project. You feel it the first time you hear the car engine. You feel it when you learn how diverse everyone's interests are under this roof, yet you see how we all strive to actively participate in these interests with each other. Regardless of the path you take to get here, from the moment you begin your life at SAPO-PETAS STUDIOS, you will see that it feels like home and that the team here is more than anything like a creative family.

Our studio thrives on a culture based on connection. That is really what family means. This is how we had fun and made this wonderful game together.
